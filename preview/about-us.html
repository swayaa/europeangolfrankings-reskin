About Us

Ted Long: Manager
europeangolfrankings
@live.com
+49 171 616 0931

For the past 30 years, Ted has been one of Europe‘s top golf coaches and has produced some of Europe‘s finest young players. His students have played on Junior Ryder Cup, Jacques Leglise Trophy, Bonallack Trophy, St. Andrews Trophy and Palmer Cup teams. His players have won many German National Championships, international titles to include a European Amateur Championship. Ted‘s students have also gone on to play on the European Tour. In 2007, Ted was honored with Teacher of the Year (Junior Golf) by the PGA of Germany. In 2014,2017, and 2019 he was honored as Teacher of the Year by the PGA of Germany. He has a degree in Computer Science from West Virginia University and is a member of the PGA of America and PGA of Germany.

Prior to turning professional, Ted worked as a Systems Analyst for the United States Army European Command in Heidelberg, Germany where he developed Command and Control Information Systems.

Gunar Petersen, Event & Data Management

Gunar joined EGR in 2014. He has a Masters Degree in Controlling and has played a vital roll in the optimisation of our data flow processes. He has taken over the responsibility of event and data management, as well as the communication between EGR and the respective international Golf Federations. Gunar is one of Germany’s top ranked amateurs and captained the Mannheim-Viernheim Seahawks Men’s Golf Team to the 2014 National Championships.
